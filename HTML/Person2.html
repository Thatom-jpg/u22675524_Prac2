Biograpgy and background

Ant-Man (Scott Lang) and The Wasp (Hope van Dyne) are Marvel superheroes who use Pym Particles to shrink and grow. Scott, a reformed thief, became Ant-Man under Hank Pym’s guidance, while Hope, a skilled fighter and scientist, took up The Wasp mantle. Together, they fight crime, explore the Quantum Realm, and play key roles in the Avengers’ battles.

Hobbies

Ant-Man (Scott Lang) enjoys: Magic Tricks – He loves learning and performing close-up magic. Spending Time with His Daughter – His biggest joy is bonding with his daughter, Cassie. Engineering & Tech – As a former electrical engineer, he enjoys tinkering with gadgets.
