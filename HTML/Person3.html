Biograpgy and background

Natasha Romanoff, aka Black Widow, is a highly skilled spy, assassin, and Avenger. Trained in the Red Room, she was once a Russian agent before defecting to S.H.I.E.L.D. and later joining the Avengers. A master of combat, espionage, and strategy, she played a crucial role in major battles, including the fight against Thanos

Hobbies

Martial Arts Training – Constantly honing her combat skills. Gymnastics & Acrobatics – A passion from her Red Room training. Driving & Vehicles – Skilled in high-speed chases and piloting.
