Biograpgy and background

Peter Parker (Spider-Man) is a young superhero from Queens, New York, who gained spider-like powers after being bitten by a radioactive spider. As Spider-Man, he fights crime while balancing his life as a high school student and later, a college student. His motto, "With great power comes great responsibility," drives his actions.

Hobbies

Photography – Peter loves taking pictures, especially of his superhero adventures. Science – He's passionate about chemistry and technology. Web-Swinging – Enjoys swinging through New York City as Spider-Man.
